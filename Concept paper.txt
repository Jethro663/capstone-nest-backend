1. PROJECT CONTEXT
Please state the rationale of your proposed Capstone Project
Learning Management Systems (LMS) are widely used in high schools to support lesson delivery,
assessment, and class management. However, most existing LMS platforms primarily focus on content
distribution and administrative functions, offering limited support for targeted academic intervention for
students who demonstrate learning difficulties.
At Gat Andres Bonifacio High School, students across all high school grade levels exhibit varying levels of
mastery in different subjects. While teachers can identify struggling students through assessments, providing
structured and timely intervention remains challenging due to limited instructional time, large class sizes, and
the absence of an integrated intervention-focused system.
This project proposes the development of Nexora, a Learning Management System with Learning
Experience Platform (LXP) features designed to support targeted student intervention for the entire high
school population of Gat Andres Bonifacio High School. The LMS serves as the primary platform for
managing classes, lessons, and assessments for all students, while the LXP functions as a performancebased intervention component.
Page 2 of 12
Access to the LXP is restricted to selected students who demonstrate learning gaps, such as those obtaining
scores below the defined performance threshold used by the system (currently below 74%). The LXP is
directly connected to the LMS to ensure continuity of instruction, allowing students to revisit previous
lessons and assessments as part of remediation. Teacher-guided recommendations generated from uploaded
modules support intervention planning, while teachers retain full control over instruction and intervention
through the LMS.

2. OBJECTIVES
State the general objective and at least 5 specific objectives of the study
General Objective:
To design and develop a web and mobile-based Learning Management System (LMS) integrated with a
Learning Experience Platform (LXP) that supports all subjects across all grade levels, provides targeted
learning intervention for selected students who demonstrate learning gaps, and assists teachers through
teacher-guided instructional support, performance-based intervention, and system evaluation.
Specific Objectives:
1. To develop an LMS that allows teachers and administrators to manage classes, students,
lessons, assessments, and learning progress effectively, including support for automatic
formatting and extraction of uploaded learning modules.
2. To integrate an LXP within the LMS that is accessible only to selected students who
demonstrate learning gaps based on the implemented intervention threshold policy,
ensuring targeted and effective
intervention.
3. To design and integrate an AI NPC mentor that assists students by explaining mistakes,
providing hints, and recommending remedial activities.
4. To conduct a system evaluation to assess the usability, functionality, performance, and user
satisfaction of the LMS–LXP integration from the perspectives of teachers and selected
students.
5. To provide teacher support features that assist educators in identifying learning gaps, managing
intervention activities through the LMS-linked LXP, and reducing instructional workload through
automated content processing.

3. SIGNIFICANCE OF THE STUDY
Discuss the importance of the project once developed
• For Students, the system provides structured academic support through targeted intervention.
Selected students are given access to the LXP, allowing them to review previous lessons, revisit
assessments, and follow guided remediation paths based on their performance. This approach
supports mastery of learning competencies and reduces learning gaps across subjects.
• For Teachers, Nexora offers clear instructional benefits. Teachers can easily upload learning
modules through automatic content extraction, identify students who require intervention, and
manage LXP activities directly through the LMS. Teacher-guided recommendations assist in
recommending remediation without replacing teacher judgment, helping reduce workload while
improving intervention effectiveness.
• For the School, the system promotes data-informed academic intervention while maintaining a
unified learning environment. The integration of LXP within the LMS allows structured remediation
without disrupting regular instruction. Optional integration of intervention results into the LMS
provides reference data for academic support, without functioning as official class records.
• For Future Researchers and Developers, the project serves as a reference for designing LMS–LXP
systems that support performance-based intervention, controlled access, and system evaluation
within a high school setting.

4. SCOPE AND DELIMITATION
Refers to the scope and inclusive frame/s of references as well as delimitation/s of the study
Scope
• The system supports all high school grade levels and subjects at Gat Andres Bonifacio High School
through an LMS.
• The Learning Experience Platform (LXP) is designed for targeted intervention and is accessible only
to selected students based on assessment performance using the implemented intervention threshold
policy (currently below 74%).
• Students are allowed to revisit previous lessons and assessments as part of the intervention
process.
• Teachers access, manage, and monitor LXP activities exclusively through the LMS.
• The system includes teacher-guided recommendations generated from uploaded learning modules to
support intervention.
• Teachers can upload modules with ease through automatic content extraction and formatting
features.
• The platform is accessible via web and mobile devices.
• System evaluation is included to assess usability, functionality, performance, and user satisfaction.
Delimitation
• The system does not replace classroom instruction or teacher decision-making.
• Full automation of grading and official class record management is excluded.
• The LXP functions solely as an intervention component and is not intended for general student use.
• Integration of intervention results into the LMS is optional and for reference purposes only.
• Advanced predictive analytics and large-scale AI model training are not included.
• Deployment beyond Gat Andres Bonifacio High School is outside the scope of the study.
• Offline functionality and third-party content integrations are excluded.

teachers, aligning with DepEd curricula for Grade 9 math. Studies show AI NPCs enhance

with Nexora, your LMS augmented with LXP features for targeted interventions at Gat Andres

identification of at-risk students to deliver adaptive support in resource-constrained educational
settings. By blending LMS structure with LXP's dynamic, learner-centric tools, Nexora embodies the
article's vision for hybrid platforms that bridge learning gaps through intuitive, school-specific
interventions.
